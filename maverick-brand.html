01 · Positioning

Brand positioning and identity

Maverick is not a general painting contractor. They are structural remediation specialists, approved applicators, and advanced coating experts. The identity should feel like engineering precision, architectural authority, and clinical trust — never generic “bakkie-and-brush” energy.

Archetype · The Engineer / The Protector

  • “The Turn-Key Asset Solution.”
  • “De-Risking Your Multi-Million Rand Asset.”

Who we speak to

  • High-net-worth homeowners

02 · Visual system

Visual style and colour

Industrial-chic, technical, and modern. Lean into blueprint and data-terminal cues — grids, mono labels, sharp accents — to separate Maverick from commodity painters.

How to use colour

Charcoal grounds everything (premium, serious, dark-mode canvas). Turquoise drives highlights, technical lines, UI borders, and primary callouts — technology and cleanliness. Green is for verification, guarantees, QA, and “approved” moments — a quick read for safety and trust.

Typography — Pair clean geometric sans (e.g. system UI stack / SF-style) with monospace for specs, labels, dates, and reporting lines so communications feel documented and accountable.

03 · Offer

Core services — the four pillars

All marketing should ladder to these four. Maverick solves problems from the structure up.

Pillar 01

Structural & remedial repairs

The foundation: concrete spalling repair, structural crack injection, expansion joint sealing, rising damp treatment.

Pillar 02

Specialized coating systems

High-performance systems: epoxy and polyurethane flooring, hygienic food-grade floors, Marmoran application, industrial corrosion control.

Pillar 03

Advanced waterproofing

High-stakes ingress: flat concrete roofs, leaking balconies (under-tile), torch-on and liquid membranes, basement waterproofing.

Pillar 04

Commercial & high-access painting

Volume and scale: body corporate painting, industrial and factory sites, rope access, roof restoration, stucco and wall coatings.

04 · Differentiator

Unique selling proposition — Independent QA

This is Maverick’s strongest marketing proof point: independent third-party QA — not self-marked homework.

  1. 1

    Substrate specification and assessment

    Scope and document the substrate before work is sold or applied.

  2. 2

    Continuous application inspection

  3. 3

    Verifiable bi-weekly reporting

    Recorded history trustees and asset owners can file — builds long-term trust.
